The Best Movies that I have watched to date

Listed below is my top 3

Heart of Stone

The best in its genre due to its humour and a combination of real action with science fiction.

Cloudy Meatballs

Just an awesome piece of animation when you want to spend some time with the kids, chilling on the couch.

The Lincoln Lawyer

Well this is a series, but I had to sneak it in here. Who doesn't like a cool story line about someone who always finds a way to win, crashing the competition.
